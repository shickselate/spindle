**ZARIM CORE CEASEFIRE DECLARED AFTER TENSE STANDOFF**

A sudden and unexpected breakthrough has occurred in the Zarim Core, a research outpost and living biome operated by the Nomads of Icarus. Following a tense standoff, Sael Zirra, Spy of Nomads of Icarus, has brokered a ceasefire between rival factions. The development comes after a strange transmission was intercepted, sparking fears of an escalation. The situation has been defused, and calm has returned to the region. Details of the mysterious transmission remain unclear.
