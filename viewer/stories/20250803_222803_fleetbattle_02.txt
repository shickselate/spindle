**GAMMA VERGE NEWS NETWORK**

**SKIRMISH ERUPTS IN GAMA VERGE, BOTH SIDES SUFFER CASUALTIES**

A brief but intense confrontation broke out in Gamma Verge yesterday, as House Talmar and Norg Syndicate patrols clashed. According to eyewitnesses, the skirmish lasted approximately five minutes before both sides disengaged. The aftermath saw five Norg vessels and three Talmar frigates destroyed or heavily damaged. No victors were declared, leaving analysts to label the exchange "meaningless." Local traders expressed relief that civilian ships were not directly involved in the conflict.
