**KEPLER SYSTEM NEWS**

**MIRACLE BIRTH ROCKS TERRAFORMING ZONE; SOCIAL UNREST ERUPTS**

A shocking development has occurred in the Kepler System's terraforming zone, as Sael Doran, Governor of The Pale Chorus, has given birth to a miracle child. The event has sparked widespread social unrest and concern among the region's inhabitants. The usually reclusive Governor's unexpected pregnancy had raised eyebrows, but the sudden appearance of the newborn has sent shockwaves throughout the system. Amidst the turmoil, it is reported that The Pale Chorus has retreated from the terraforming zone to regroup and assess the situation.
