**GALACTIC TIMES**

**NORG OFFICIALS ARREST ZHEN AGENT FOR FUSION CORE THEFT**

A Zhen Collective agent was taken into custody yesterday while attempting to extract sensitive fusion core schematics from Solace Prime. Norg officials condemned the act, stating "We will not tolerate theft masquerading as diplomacy." The Zhen delegation has denied involvement, claiming it was a "tragic misunderstanding". The incident has sparked tensions between the two species, with diplomatic efforts uncertain in its aftermath.
