**GALACTIC TIMES**

**VIREL ENCLAVE ADMIRAL JALEN MYRR DELIVERS WARNING AFTER ICARUS DRIFT DUEL**

Asteroid Field, Icarus Drift - Admiral Jalen Myrr of Virel Enclave has emerged from a high-stakes honour duel in the ancient ruins region of the Icarus Drift. The encounter was marked by complications, including a false signal that threatened to upend the proceedings. Despite this setback, Admiral Myrr successfully delivered a warning to the dueling party, bringing an end to the engagement. Details of the duel and its outcome are still being gathered. Virel Enclave officials have confirmed the Admiral's safe return.
