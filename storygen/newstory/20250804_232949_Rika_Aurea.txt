**GALACTIC TIMES**

**Vault of Echoes Leadership Change Fails Amid Solar Flare**

Kaelin-IV - A sudden solar flare disrupted diplomatic efforts to establish a new leadership in the uncontacted Vault of Echoes, a gas giant moon. Rika Aurea, Diplomat of House Myrrk, had been sent to facilitate the transition, but an experiment aimed at stabilizing the region's power dynamics went awry due to the intense energy surge. The failed endeavor leaves the Vault's future uncertain, with tensions potentially exacerbated by the sudden reversal.
