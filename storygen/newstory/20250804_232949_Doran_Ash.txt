**ION REACH TIMES**

**PROPHETIC VISION ROCKS TRADE NEXUS**

In a shocking turn of events, Fleet Commander Doran Ash of House Myrrk reported experiencing a prophetic vision in the Ion Reach asteroid belt yesterday. The normally stable region has seen an uptick in trade and commerce recently, making the sudden disturbance all the more unexpected. According to sources close to Ash, the vision was unclear but seemed to hint at significant changes on the horizon. The exact nature of these changes remains unknown, leaving many wondering what consequences this may have for the galaxy's busiest trade route.
