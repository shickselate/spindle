**HOUSE TALMAR, NOVA FREEHOLD SEAL FIVE-CYCLE TRADE PACT**

In a surprise move, House Talmar and Nova Freehold have agreed to a five-cycle trade pact, effective immediately. Under the terms of the agreement, Nova Freehold will provide House Talmar with 30 metric tons of high-grade ore each cycle, while House Talmar will supply tactical drones on a monthly basis. The deal has sparked speculation among analysts, with commentator Teva Lun suggesting that House Talmar's acquisition of these advanced drones may be indicative of larger strategic plans.
