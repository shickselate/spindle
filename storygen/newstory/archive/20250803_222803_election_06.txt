**CORE TIMES**

**HISTORIC ELECTION SEES ALYRA DORNE AS NEW CORE PRESIDENT**

In a decisive vote, the Assembly has elected Alyra Dorne of House Talmar as the new Core President. With 54% of the total votes cast, Dorne secured the top spot in her first bid for the position. In her inaugural address, she emphasized the importance of responsible leadership, stating, "We are not rulers of stars. We are their stewards." Her victory grants her significant powers over war declarations and galactic tariffs, positioning her to shape the course of interstellar relations.
